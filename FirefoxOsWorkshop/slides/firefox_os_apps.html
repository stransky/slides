Why Firefox OS?

  • It's new - you can be the first one
  • It's fast - do it today
  • It's easy - HTML+JS
  • It's free - don't need to ask anyone to publish your app

Firefox OS vs. Android

  • No hw back button - app provides it
  • Applications can't quit
  • Multitasking (gestures)

App types

  • Hosted apps vs. packaged apps
  • System APIs
    • Hosted
    • Privileged - approved on Martetplace
    • Certified - native ones
